Introduction

In the past couple of years, the American entertainment industry took a massive turn. It was found

that younger generations were not as interested in traditional forms of media as previous generations.

A new industry that is begining to emerge is eSports, or Competitive Gaming. eSports is seen by many

as a flop ready to happen, haver we are compiling this report to compare this new industry to a pre-existing

industy, traditional sports, through statistics.

We decided to chose this topic so that we could indicate the rise in eSports,

and how they could possibly surpass traditional sports in terms of viewership.
